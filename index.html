About Garden

Mukebhai Recreation Garden in Shirpur is a popular destination for families and nature lovers. The garden features lush green lawns, a variety of flowering plants, walking paths, and dedicated play areas for children. It serves as a peaceful retreat for relaxation, morning walks, and community gatherings. The garden is well-maintained and offers a serene environment for visitors to enjoy the beauty of nature.

Specifications

  • Jogging tracks for fitness enthusiasts
  • Dedicated play areas for children
  • Beautiful fountains and water features
  • Lush green lawns and landscaped gardens
  • Variety of flowering and ornamental plants
  • Benches and shaded seating zones
  • Walking paths for peaceful strolls
  • Clean and well-maintained environment
  • Ample parking space for visitors
  • Security and safety measures in place
  • Restrooms and drinking water facilities
  • Open spaces for yoga and group activities
  • Eco-friendly initiatives and recycling bins

Time

The garden is open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily.

Visitors can enjoy the garden during these hours, making it a perfect spot for morning walks, evening strolls, and family outings.

Note: The garden may have special timings during festivals or events, so it's advisable to check for any announcements or updates.

For more information, you can contact the garden management.
